COLORA

How do I play colora? Tell me now!

Easy. There are different colors with their respective numbers.

Here they are:

And there are 3 boxes. Press [PLAY] and the boxes will randomly rotate the colors.

Press [STOP] to stop each box from changing colors.

  • If three boxes are equal, you got 200 points and a new background image from Flickr
  • If two boxes are equal and next to each other, you got 100 points.
  • If two boxes are equal and separated from each other, you got 50 points.
  • If all boxes are different you got no points... obviously.

Every time you reach 1000 points the level increases by one and a new color is added to the list... and the speed of the color rotation increases!
